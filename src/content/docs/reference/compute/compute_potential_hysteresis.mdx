### Criteria for hysteresis
<Steps>
1. At least three time points that have $\Delta$QTc greater than 5 ms
2. $\lvert T_{\text{max}} - U_{\text{max}} \rvert \geq 1 \text{ hr}$, where $T_{\text{max}}$ is time of maximal concentration, and $U_{\text{max}}$ is time of maximal $\Delta$QTc
</Steps>
